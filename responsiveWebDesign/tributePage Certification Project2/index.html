Linus Torvalds

The man who gave us Linux and Git, and indirectly, Android and the Internet

  • 1969- Torvalds was born in Finland to journalists Anna and Nils Torvalds.
  • 1981- Linus' interest in computers began with a VIC-20.
  • 1988-1996- Attended the University of Helsinki, graduating with a masters in computer science.
  • 1991- The first Linux protoypes were publicly published on the internet from an FTP server at the University of Helsinki.
  • 1969- Redhat and VA-Linux presented Torvalds with stock options in gratitude for this creations. That year, both companies went public and the value of Torvald's stocks briefly went up to $ 20 million.
  • 2006- Around 20% of the Linux code is written by Torvalds, which is still the largest percentage.
  • 2005- Torvalds began work on the Git version control software.
